My First Game

For my very first game, I wanted to make something that was something that I would play at home. I used to play piano
tiles everyday and became addicted to just improving my score. I love the piano tiles games and decided to make my very
own version of it. If you have never heard of piano tiles, it is a very simple game. In this game, there will be piano
tiles falling down from the top of the screen. The objective is to click the tile when it appears on screen as fast as
possible before it hits the bottom of the screen. If the piano tile hits the bottom, the game ends.
